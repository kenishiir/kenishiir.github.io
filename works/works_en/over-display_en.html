Akira Wakita, Over Display - Modality

This work was announced at "Symptom Visualized" third solo exhibition by Akira Wakita. By using the display against the original way of use, it reminds us that we are bound by the original rules and customs.

In this work, I was charge of part of the artifacts stuck on the display.

2018.1, mixed media
