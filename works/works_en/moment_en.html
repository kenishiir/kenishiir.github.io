Moment

A sculpture with a time theme. We measured the wind at the venue of DESIGN TOUCH 2019. The measured data which cannot be captured by the human eye was visualized and expressed as a sculpture.

" When the concept of 'moment', which is usually elusive, appears in front of us as a physical object, the viewer will reconsider the meaning of 'time' ".
(Translated from TOKYO MIDTOWN DESIGN TOUCH 2019, Moment | DESIGN TOUCH 2019 ).
