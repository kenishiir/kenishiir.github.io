Narrative Phenomenon

This work is an abstract visualisation of the stories we tell everyday by giving them movements and behaviours.

Each group of lines flowing represents an event with different element happening in a world with time and space. Each elements of the event are being told in different way from a different point of view. Thus, each event runs becomes a narration running through a world of storytelling.
Based on the "Narrative Discourse" of Gérard Genette (1930-2018).
